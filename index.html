About Our Recommended menu

Bluemoon

The best beverage to end your day is a Blue Flower Pea Tea with Jasmine. Recommend that no sugar or cream is added. It taste like a regular Jasmine Tea because without Jasmine it taste like regular hot water.

Great White

The best beverage to start your day is a Great White Coffee. Recommend that you use cream and sugar to add flavor into it. Second selection is to ask for an Ice Great White Coffee with milk. Once you've tasted our Great White Coffee, you may want to return here the next day.

Green Nature

The best beverage to drink after a large meal or sweet desserts is Green Tea. Recommend that no sugar or anything else is added so you can feel the calm nature inside your body even if it taste bitter.

Imperial White

The best beverage to drink after a large meal or sweet desserts is a White Tea. Recommend that no sugar or anything else is added so you can feel the calmness in you even if it taste bitter. It's not as bitter as Green Tea.

Truffle Fries

Try out our Fries mixed with parmesan cheese that can melt in your mouth. Once you've tasted this fries it will make your mind blow.

Beefy Sandwich

The chef work so hard to make the Sandwich very flavorful in our mouth. It takes a lot of effort to make this Beef Sandwich with double cheese. The taste of this Beef Sandwich will make your mind pop with the ingredients that are all put there.

Cheesyham

Since most Croissant are popular for sweet pastries and desserts the taste of this Cheesyham Croissant will surprise you how it melts in your mouth. It have eggs as an optional choice but it will charge you Rp 25,000. This is one of chefs favourite recipe.

Ultimeatum

Chefs number one favourite is the Ultimeatum Burger. You will be surprise of how this Burger taste because these two Patties is that one is Beef and one is Venison. Recommend that you choose Medium Rare so it will taste soft and melts in your mouth. Once you've tasted this Ultimeatum Burger you will be delighted.
